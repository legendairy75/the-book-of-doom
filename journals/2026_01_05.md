## compressed archives (.tar.gz)
- When you encounter a *.tar.gz* file you first unpack it, then de compress it
	- `gunzip file.tar.gz`
	- `tar xvf file.tar`
- ### zcat
	- the above methed is in effisent. a better way to handle compressed archives is *zcat* and piping it to tar
		- `zcat file.tar.gz | tar xvf -`
		- *zcat* works exactly like *gunzip -dc*
			- -d for decompress
			- -c sends resilt to stdout
- becaus zcat is so common tar has a *-z* flag to automadicaly invoke zcat
	- `tar ztvf file.tar.gz`
- There is also *zip* that works with windows archives
